About PolarisOS

A love letter to the early days of the desktop metaphor and the time where the internet was a series of distributed but loosely connected spaces.

Development

This website was written with Vue.js built with Vite. I chose vue because it allows me to organise and reuse my code very efficiently and I can easily add interactivity to little parts of my website/application.

While I understand it might be a little bit against the spirit of writing every piece of HTML yourself, it gives me so much flexibility that I cannot refuse that! :-)

Also, given I'm an old lad; I was there, doing that back in the day. I've walked the talk.

Your data

This site has no server backend and is a totally static. Your "settings" are saved on your device in the browsers Local storage. There are no trackers, there are no third party requests other than loading the fonts from Googles CDN. (Only if you select other fonts than the default sans-serif and serif.)
